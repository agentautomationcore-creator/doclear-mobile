How to test:
1. Open the app and tap "Scan my first document"
2. Accept the AI analysis consent
3. Complete the short onboarding (language, country, status)
4. On Scanner tab, tap "Camera" to photograph any document
   OR tap "Import file" to select a PDF
5. AI will analyze the document (10-20 seconds)
6. View analysis: Health Score, Key Facts, Risks, Actions
7. Tap "Ask a question" to chat with AI about the document
8. Try "Translate" on any document
9. Switch languages in Profile (10 languages supported)
10. Arabic activates RTL layout

Requirements:
- Camera required for document scanning
- Internet connection required for AI analysis
- iOS 16.0 or later
